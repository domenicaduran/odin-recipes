Soft Tortilla Tacos

Description

This is one of my favorite recipes because of it's simplicity and versatility. Everyone in your family will be able to enjoy this recipe by tweaking the toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 4 Russet Potatoes
  • Vegetable oil
  • Salt
  • Taco Seasoning
  • At least a dozen small soft tortillas or hard taco shells
  • Optional Items
    • Can of corn
    • Can of black beans
    • Grated cheese
    • Salsa
    • Sour cream
    • Shredded lettuce and other garnish

Instructions

  1. Dice potatoes into chunks slightly smaller than sugar cubes
  2. Heat a large pan that has a lid to medium heat.
  3. Add a table spoon of vegetable oil then add diced potatoes to the pan.
  4. Add salt to taste and taco seasoning or red chile powder also to taste.
  5. Cover and cook potatoes for about ten minutes or until soft.
  6. You can uncover them for a few minutes in order to crisp the outside of the potatoes a little.
  7. While potatoes are cooking, brown then drain your ground beef
    • You can also add seasoning to taste to your ground beef.
  8. After ground beef is browned and drained and potatoes are soft, combine them in a large skillet.
  9. Season to taste again.
  10. At this point you can optionally add a can of corn or a can of drained black beans.
    • This is great if you need to cook for a large amount of people and have these pantry staples on hand.
  11. Heat the tortillas either in the microwave or on the stove a few at a time as needed before serving.
  12. At this point you can put together your tacos with whatever optional garnish you'd like.
  13. Enjoy!
