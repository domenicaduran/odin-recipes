Pressure Cooker Pulled Chicken

Description

This recipe requires a pressure cooker. You can also make this in a slow cooker but will have to increase the hours. But this is an easy and quick recipe to make at the end of a work day.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 Chicken Breasts.
  • 1-2 Cups of Barbeque Sauce of your choosing
  • Water or Chicken Broth
  • Recommended/Optional ingredients
    • Bread, or buns.
    • Sliced pickles
    • Diced Onions
    • Lettuce
