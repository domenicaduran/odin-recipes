Pancakes

Description

This is an easy pancake recipe that I'm sure I found on the internet elsewhere and have since written it on a note card. But its really easy and uses ingredients you most likely have on hand.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 cups flour
  • 3.5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1.25 cups of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix together dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Combine all wet ingredients together, and then slowly mix the wet ingredients into the dry ingredients.
  3. The consistency should not be too thick or too runny.
  4. Spray a skillet (medium heat) with cooking spray and pour batter into 3-4 inch diameter circles
  5. Cook for 1-2 minutes on each side and flip over.
  6. Butter right away and serve with syrup.
  7. It should make 6-8 medium sized pancakes.
